Preventing Cyber Attacks While Streaming

There are many precautions you can take in order to secure your account and data. Keeping your data away from hackers is crucial in today's society. Check down below for some tips!

Tips for Keeping Your Data Secure

  • Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on all streaming platforms.
  • Use strong and unique passwords that relate to who you are.
  • Do not click on random links that people broadcast in text-chats.
  • Frequently update and check your systems software/antivirus.
  • Keep personal information to yourself.
